What is HoosExchange?

HoosExchange is an online thriftshop for CVille locals and UVA students. HoosExchange was created at the 2023 HooHacks Hackathon with the intention of offering a way for students and CVille locals to easily buy and sell used clothes, shoes, and accessories - all while making a positive environmental impact. Post some of your clothes on HoosExchange today, and see what an impact it has!

The Team:

The team consists of UVA Hoos, Sai Singamsetty and Jacob Hobbs. Both Virginia Beach natives, Sai and Jacob thrift in their free time, but were always seeking online options, as well as ways to connect more with their fellow UVA students and neighbors. Sai and Jacob are also interdisciplinary computer science majors, looking for ways to use computer science to better the world around them.
